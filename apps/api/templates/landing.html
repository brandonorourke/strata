Most tools summarize what you already see.

News aggregation and covenant tracking are commodity layers — every platform has them. The harder, more valuable problem is reading the primary records themselves: the registry filing, the docket entry, the regulatory action — and correctly resolving who they're actually about across sources and over time.

That's what we build: one entity-resolved view per company, assembled from records that are public but hard to monitor, with a citation back to the source on every event.
